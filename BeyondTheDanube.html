Beyond The Danube

The Project

Beyond The Danube aims to let you step into the life of the Italian scientist Luigi Ferdinando Marsili and experience his life in an immersive and interactive way.

◆
The Figure

Soldier, Scientist, Explorer

He is the founder of the Bologna Institute of Sciences, which represents one of the most revolutionary milestones not only for his life but for the whole city of Bologna itself. The foundation, structure, and scientific methodology of it was profoundly shaped by Marsili's connection to the Royal Society of London.

◆
His Life

Marsili's Legacy Lives On

Marsili's Museum is located within the historic Palazzo Poggi in Bologna. It preserves the extraordinary collections gathered by the scientist throughout his lifetime.

Today it stands as a vibrant testament to Marsili's vision of knowledge as a shared, universal pursuit.

Palazzo Poggi, Via Zamboni 33, Bologna
