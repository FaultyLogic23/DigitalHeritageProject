The Project

Beyond The Danube aims to let you step in the life of the italian scientist Luigi Ferdinando Marsili and experience his life in an immersive and interactive way.

The Figure

Soldier, Scientist, Explorer

He is the founder of the Bologna Institute of Sciences, which represents one of the most revolutionary milestones in his life. The foundation, structure, and scientifics methodology of it were profoundly shaped by Marsili's connection to the Royal Society of London.
